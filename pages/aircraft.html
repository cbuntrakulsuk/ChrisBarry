Aircraft Parts


Our Aircraft Parts Division is dedicated to providing factory new, new surplus and overhauled certified items to our customers. All material meets OEM and FSN specifications.

Chrisbarry Aircraft Corporation aircraft, helicopter and overhaul capability is extensive. We are ready to support many aircraft (fixed-wing) or helicopter (rotary-wing) whether it be commercial, government, military or government use, or even private operators.

With over 30 years experience and expertise of our techical and engineering staff , all parts are carefully inspected before shipment and checked to ensure they meet the critera set by the government and/or aircraft manufacturers.
