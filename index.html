About us

With over 30 years in the industry and an open rating license for Accessory Class 1, 2, and 3 from the FAA, Consolidated Aircraft Supply Co., Inc.'s maintenance, repair and overhaul capability is extensive. We are ready to support any aircraft (fixed-wing) or helicopter (rotary-wing) whether it be commercial (airline), government (military and/or public use), or private operators.

Combining the experience and expertise of our technical and engineering staff, all parts are carefully inspected before shipment and checked to be sure they meet the criteria set by the Government and/or Aircraft manufacturers.
